If that title doesn't show you how much I love comics ...

Everyone likes to ask me, "Karman, which do you like more? Marvel or DC?" to to which I answer with nervous chuckles. Because how does one really measure which they like more?

Instead of answering such a preposterous question, I'll list all my favorite superheroes! Well. I guess not all, because, well because that would take all day!

  • Batman

Batman (DC) does not possess any superpowers; rather, he relies on his genius intellect, physical prowess, martial arts abilities, detective skills, etc.

  • Captain America

Captain America (Marvel) has agility, strength, speed, endurance, and reaction time superior to any Olympic athlete who ever competed. The Super-Soldier formula that he has metabolized has enhanced all of his bodily functions to the peak of human efficiency.

  • Professor X

Professor X (Marvel) possesses vast mental powers, including telepathy; the ability to induce in others mental illusions, temporary mental or physical paralysis, and loss of specific memories or total amnesia.

  • Superman

Superman (DC) possesses the powers of flight, superhuman strength, x-ray vision, heat vision, cold breath, super-speed, and enhanced hearing. Superman is immensely strong both in terms of muscle power and ability to take physical punishment.

  • Raven

Raven (DC) possesses vast magical powers, such as emotional manipulation; psychic power; shadow manipulation; supernatural knowledge; telekinesis; teleportation; flight; and healing abilities.

  • Wolverine

Wolverine is a mutant who possesses animal-keen senses, enhanced physical capabilities, and powerful regenerative ability known as a healing factor.
